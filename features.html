Getting Started

Features

watchAny is built around one idea: play anything, track everything. Here's what it does.

🎬
Hardware-Accelerated Player
Powered by libmpv. Handles 4K, HDR, Dolby Vision, HEVC 10-bit, AV1, and virtually any codec without buffering.
📺
Anime Dashboard
Browse Trending, Top Rated, Seasonal, and Continue Watching rails. Pulls live data from AniList API.
🎥
Movies & Series
Full TMDB integration. Discover new releases, popular titles, and track your watch history.
📖
Manga Reader
Browse and read manga with chapter tracking, bookmarks, and AniList progress sync built in.
🌊
Torrent Playback
Built-in TorrServer streams magnet links directly. Start watching while the torrent downloads — no client needed.
📡
HLS & DASH Streams
Paste any m3u8 or mpd URL and start playing instantly. Works with most IPTV and streaming sources.
🔗
AniList Sync
Connect your AniList account to sync your library, track episode progress, and update scores automatically.
🔌
HStream Extensions
Load Keiyoushi-compatible anime extension sources to stream anime from aggregated providers.

Player Capabilities

  • Subtitle support — SRT, ASS/SSA, embedded subtitles, external files
  • Audio tracks — switch between dubbed/subbed tracks mid-playback
  • Chapter navigation — skip to any chapter with a single click
  • Playback speed — 0.25× to 4× without pitch distortion
  • Resume playback — automatically resumes from where you left off
  • Episode switching — next/prev episode controls in the player
  • Download manager — queue downloads from the player directly
  • Screen lock — keep display awake during playback (Android)

Anime Dashboard Details

The anime home page surfaces content from AniList in horizontal railway tracks:

  • Continue Watching — episodes you paused, with progress bars and hover-to-delete
  • Trending Now — live trending anime from AniList with infinite scroll
  • Top Rated — highest scoring series of all time
  • Current Season — what's airing right now
  • Popular Movies — top-rated anime films
  • Upcoming — not yet aired but announced titles

Each card shows the poster, score badge, and progress indicator. Click to open the full detail page with episode list, synopsis, genres, studio, and stream/torrent options.

💡 Hover over any card in the Continue Watching rail to reveal a delete button — click it to remove the item from your history.
