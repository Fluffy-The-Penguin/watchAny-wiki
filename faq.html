FAQ

Common questions answered. If you don't find your answer here, open an issue on GitHub.

Is watchAny free? ↓
Yes — completely free and open source. No ads, no subscriptions, no premium tier. The source code is on GitHub.
Windows says the installer is unsafe. Is it? ↓
No. The warning appears because the app is not code-signed (signing certificates cost hundreds of dollars per year). Click "More info" → "Run anyway" to proceed. You can verify the binary yourself — the full source code is public.
Do I need an AniList account? ↓
No. You can browse anime, play torrents, and use all features without an account. An AniList account is only needed if you want to sync your library, watch progress, and ratings to AniList.
Which Android APK should I download? ↓
For most modern phones (2017 and newer), download app-arm64-v8a-release.apk. For older devices, use app-armeabi-v7a-release.apk. The x86_64 APK is only for emulators.
Video is choppy / not playing smoothly. What do I do? ↓
  • Ensure your GPU drivers are up to date
  • For 4K content, make sure your system meets the requirements
  • Try a different stream quality or source
  • For torrents, wait for more data to buffer before seeking
How do I remove an item from Continue Watching? ↓
Hover over any card in the Continue Watching rail and click the ✕ button that appears in the top-left corner. This immediately removes it from your history.
Why does the app say "Update Available" even after updating? ↓
This was a bug fixed in v2.0.34. Update to the latest version and the prompt will no longer appear incorrectly.
Does watchAny work on macOS or Linux? ↓
Not officially. watchAny is built with Flutter and could theoretically run on macOS and Linux, but no official builds are provided. You're welcome to compile from source and open a PR if you get it working.
How do I report a bug or request a feature? ↓
Open an issue on the GitHub Issues page. Include your OS, app version, and steps to reproduce.
